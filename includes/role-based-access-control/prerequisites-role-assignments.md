- `Microsoft.Authorization/roleAssignments/write` 和 `Microsoft.Authorization/roleAssignments/delete` 權限，例如[使用者存取系統管理員](../../articles/role-based-access-control/built-in-roles.md#user-access-administrator)或[擁有者](../../articles/role-based-access-control/built-in-roles.md#owner)
